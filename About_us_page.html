Our Story

How a simple idea transformed into Sri Lanka's favorite travel platform

From Passion to Platform

Tripzly was born in 2020 from a shared passion for travel and technology. Our founders, avid travelers themselves, recognized the challenges travelers faced when planning trips in Sri Lanka. They envisioned a platform that would simplify travel planning while highlighting the incredible diversity of experiences our beautiful country has to offer.

What started as a small startup with just three passionate individuals has grown into a team of 50 travel enthusiasts, tech experts, and customer service professionals. Our journey has been filled with exciting milestones, from our first 100 bookings to being featured in Travel Magazine as Sri Lanka's most innovative travel platform.

Today, Tripzly connects thousands of travelers with unique stays, unforgettable experiences, and authentic local adventures across Sri Lanka. We're proud to have helped over 100,000 travelers discover the magic of our island nation.
