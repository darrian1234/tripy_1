Weather Alerts & Travel Advice

Stay informed about weather conditions that may affect your travel plans in Sri Lanka.

High Advisory - Southern Coast

Monsoon conditions expected through the weekend. Heavy rainfall and strong winds may affect coastal areas. Avoid beach activities and boat tours.

Moderate Advisory - Hill Country

Possible landslides in hilly areas after heavy rainfall. Exercise caution when traveling through mountain roads.

Low Advisory - Northern Regions

High temperatures expected. Stay hydrated and avoid outdoor activities during peak sun hours (11am-3pm).
